Who are we?


We are locally owned an operated since 1965 and the ties we carry with the community run deep. The locals know us and love us but we recently have decided to work on growing our businesses. Our number one goal has always been to provide to our customers the freshest foods available grown locally and imported and they love us for it. With our new website up and running we hope to attract more business through our popular fresh fruit smoothies and drinks that can be purchased online. Moving Forward we look forward to growing Bountiful Foods and continuing to serve as many customers in the best way we know how.
