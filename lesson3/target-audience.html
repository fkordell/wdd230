Target-Audience

The target audience for this chamber of commerce website is anyone in the area who is looking for more information on businesses, history, attractions, events or ways to network and be involved in the community. This could include anyone from residents, businesses, people who are new to the area or people looking to actively serve or participate in the community.

Personas

Elias is a 35 year old native Idahoan resident and is familiar with many areas and has been an active participant in the communities he has lived in previously. Being new to American Falls, Elias wants to continue that pattern and so the Chamber of Commerce site proves to be really helpful to him in learning more about the community and what is available.


Sarah is a 28 year old ambitious business owner who runs her own hair salon. She has built quite a name for herself within her friend group but wants to find new ways of expanding her sphere of clientel around the community. She knows that networking with other business owners could present new opportunites for her little business to expand.

Scenarios

1. Sarah connects with another business owner near her own and comes to find out that the chamber of commerce site is the way that other business owner connects with other businesses and finds out about local events where they can get involved. Sarah sees this as a great opportunity to find out about events and networking opportunities.


2. Elias having been involved in previous communities he has lived in already knows that the chamber of commerce site is a good place to go for information about the community and finding ways to get involved in community events and service opportunites. Elias is able to utilize the chamber of commerce site to find out about a town meeting coming up.


3. A business owner from a neighboring town wants to look into expanding their business into neighboring communities. They find that the best place to network and find information on businesses and business owners for this town is on the chamber of commerce website.


4. One of the town residents wants to find out more about the towns history and current leadership. They are curious to look into the roots of the town and research how it all started. They are able to find information from historical records and accounts on the chamber of commerce website.


5. Several of the towns residents and business owners want to put on a community event to help inspire tourism and business in the town and in neighboring communities. They are able to find all of the necessary information on how to host such an event and where it is allowed.
