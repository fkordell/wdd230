News Release

The American Falls city council met last Wednesday to decide on moving forward with plans to erect a new park following a city levee that was just passed the previous month. Finalizing of plans will take a few more weeks and then ground breaking should be happening soon afterwards.

Thankful Transport

Local businesses have partnered together to have public transportation for those who struggle to make it to work due to transportation issues. We are grateful for the transport that these companies provide so that all of our companies and local residents can benefit and profit from this.

Code Extravaganza

American Falls school district is proud to sponsor its 2nd annual code extravaganza. Although most residents and studednts are more familiar with farming and other blue collar jobs, the school district has seen an increasing trend for the need to learn new technologies including software and web development. The school will be hosting after school sessions on Tuesday's and Thursday's for the next 3 months.

Save our forest

Although American Falls is rich in farming history and industry, the community desires to work with the local and state government to help protect and preserve its forest lands from those trying to buy it up.
