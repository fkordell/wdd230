Ribbon Cutting

A ribbon cutting ceremony will be held Saturday afternoon at the American Falls city park for a new event center that has just been finished. This ceremony will feature a short history of the start to finish with key contributors highlighted. That will be followed by some tours of the facility as well as food and beverages for purchase.
